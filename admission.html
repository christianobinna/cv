MODE OF ADMISSION :
The convent conducts examinations and interviews for candiates seeking for admission. The first examination is organized by the COUNCIL FOR SEMINARIES & CONVENTS IN THE SOUTH EASTERN STATES OF NIGERIA (Anglican Communion). Candiates who excel go for the second and third interviews organized by the Deans of Examination and quality control unit. Applicants for higher classes apply in writing to the proprietor (The Diocesan) after attaining the basic requirements of at least 60% average from their previous schools, before an invitation for examination and interviews.

BEGINING AND END OF TERM:

  1. On the re-opening day, after a holiday or mid-term break, all students must return to school latest at 4:00pm. there should be roll-call at 5:000pm .Those who return late shall be punished appropriately.
  2. All students luggage should be searched . All unauthorized personal effects shall be confiscated and may not be returned to the owner.
  3. All fees must be paid in full before arrival.
  4. No student is allowed to leave the school before the last day of the term. special permission must be obtained in case of emergency


PAYMENT OF FEES
All fees/levies are to be paid through the designated seminary bank account at least one week before resumption and tellers changed for receipts. the allocation of students's corner or distribution of any item is based on the payment of fees i.e first-pay-first -serve. Studentship of any student is certified and guaranteed by the payment of school fees for that term or academic year.
