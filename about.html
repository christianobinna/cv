...History Of Ebony Kids Int. Nnewi
..
,,,

Ebony kids International School was established in Nnewi in 2010 by Mrs. Ezeokoli Ogechukwu. The school was initially in a rented apartment with nursery one to primary three and got its accreditation by Anambra State ministry of education in 2011. Since its opening, the school dynamic educational environment has continued to envolve. In 2014 the school moves to its permanent site of three story building. The school grew from strength to strength which gave rise to Ebony International Secondary School with the aim of extending its values to adolescents rather than kids alone. It looked as if the establishment of Ebony kids School as its name began to ring a bell to the ears of all especially those that understand what true education is. With the help of its super leaders, the school has been able to achieve chains of successes and can boast of different examination like- National and State common entrance, BECE, WAEC, NECO being taken in the school. The school also looks forward to starting a jamb centre in the school in no distant time. To add to its achievement and to lessen the burden of home training, Ebony boarding house was established since 2015.the hostel has not seized to nurture pupils and students whose homes are quite far from the school. It is worth noting that since 2014 Ebony kids school has been graduating their pupils and has crowed it with the graduation of ss3 students this year. The more Ebony pupils/students grow in population and knowledge, the more giant, beautiful and comfortable buildings are constructed. No wonder Ebony kids international school is now a world of its own that children, parents and guardians in Nnewi and Nigeria at large fight to be associated with. Compiled by Mrs.Ezeokoli Ogechukwu Proprietress
